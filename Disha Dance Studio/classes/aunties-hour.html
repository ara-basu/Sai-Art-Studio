About this class

Aunties' Hour is the studio's most beloved group — and for good reason. Designed for women aged 30 and above, this midweek evening class is a warm, high-energy celebration of Bollywood dance. No mirrors, no judgement, just movement.

Many members have been with the group for years, making it as much a social gathering as a dance class. Routines draw from classic Bollywood, Punjabi folk, and devotional styles — songs everyone in the room already knows and loves.

What to expect

A gentle warm-up with joint mobility exercises and light stretching, adapted for adult bodies.

Fun, accessible choreography to beloved Bollywood and folk songs — learned in small sections so nobody falls behind.

A relaxed pace with plenty of repetition so steps become second nature over the term.

Optional performance at the studio's annual family showcase — a highlight for the whole group.

Genuine camaraderie — this class has its own WhatsApp group and occasional chai meetups!

What to bring

Comfortable clothes you can move freely in — salwar kameez, leggings, anything works.

Flat shoes or bare feet — avoid heels or thick-soled trainers.

A water bottle and your best energy!
