About this group

The Performance Team is Disha Dance Studio's elite unit — the group behind the studio's 47 regional awards and featured appearances at events including the NASCAR Pride Celebration and the Ohio Arts & Culture Gala.

Members are selected by audition and commit to a rigorous rehearsal schedule that builds not just technique but true performance artistry. This is the group for dancers who want to compete, perform publicly, and push themselves to a professional standard.

What to expect

Intensive 90-minute rehearsals focused on precision, synchronisation, and stage presence.

Multiple choreographies per year — competition pieces, event sets, and showcase routines.

Video analysis sessions to identify and correct alignment, spacing, and expression issues.

Participation in regional competitions, cultural festivals, and private events across Columbus and Ohio.

A high-commitment, high-reward environment built on mutual respect and shared excellence.

How to join

Auditions are held at the start of each term. We look for strong foundational technique, coachability, reliability, and a genuine passion for Bollywood performance. Prior experience in one of our other classes is an advantage but not mandatory.
